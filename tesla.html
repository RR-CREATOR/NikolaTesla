Early Curiosity and Exploration

1856 - 1970s

Tesla's childhood curiosity and innate ability to visualize concepts set the stage for his journey into science and innovation. Growing up in Smiljan, Croatia, his unique perspective on the natural world laid the foundation for his later accomplishments.

Embrace of New Horizons

1880s

Tesla's immigration to the United States marked a turning point in his life. Collaborating with Edison and Westinghouse, he transitioned from an ambitious engineer to an independent inventor, embarking on a path that would redefine technology.

AC vs. DC: War of Currents

1880s - 1890s

Tesla's pivotal role in advocating for alternating current (AC) systems over direct current (DC) in the "War of Currents" against Thomas Edison revolutionized electricity distribution. His support for AC brought about safer and more efficient power transmission.

Wardenclyffe Vision Unleashed

1900s

Tesla's visionary projects, including the Wardenclyffe Tower, showcased his fascination with wireless communication and energy transmission. Although hindered by financial constraints, his efforts heralded the future potential of global connectivity.

Electromagnetic Resonance Experiments

1890s - 1910s

Tesla's relentless pursuit of electromagnetic resonance yielded revolutionary discoveries and innovations. His exploration of resonant circuits and coils laid the groundwork for numerous technologies beyond his time.

Legacy and Enduring Influence

Posthumous

Tesla's legacy endures as a testament to his brilliance and foresight. Despite his passing, his ideas continue to shape modern technology, from electricity and communication to renewable energy solutions, leaving an indelible mark on the world.
